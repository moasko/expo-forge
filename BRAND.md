# 🔥 Expo Forge - Brand Identity

## 🏷️ Brand Name: **Expo Forge**

### Why "Forge"?
- **Forge** = to create or shape something with skill and effort
- **Forge** = a place where metal is heated and shaped
- **Forge** = implies craftsmanship, strength, and transformation
- **Forge** = short, memorable, and powerful

### 🎯 Brand Values
- **Craftsmanship** - Building high-quality apps
- **Strength** - Bulletproof architecture
- **Transformation** - Turning ideas into reality
- **Modernity** - Latest technologies and practices

### 🔥 Brand Colors
- **Primary**: Orange/Red (#FF6B35) - Fire, energy, forging
- **Secondary**: Dark Gray (#2D3748) - Strength, reliability
- **Accent**: Blue (#4299E1) - Technology, trust
